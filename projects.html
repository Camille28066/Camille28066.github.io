Shedding Light on the Night

I investigate how artificial light is reshaping our nightscapesâ€”tracking its spread across countries, cities, and ecosystems, both in space and over the course of the night. Using ground-based photometers and satellite imagery, I reveal how the night is not only getting brighter but also shifting in color, with cooler, blue-rich spectra increasingly replacing the natural darkness essential for many forms of life.
