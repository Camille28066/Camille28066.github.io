Israel

Walking across the Desert of Israel ~ about 450 km on foot.

The way goes from the shores of the Red Sea to the Judean Desert, through the Negev, the Arava Valley, and the Dead Sea.

It partly follows the Incense route, an ancient trade route that connected the Arabian Peninsula to the Mediterranean. As you go, you stumble upon a few reminders of this past, and upon the occasional camel.

I started the trip a bit late in the season (March), which meant dealing with full-on desert sun, so the whole trip was about starting walking before the sun comes high, finding shade, staying hydrated, and watching the stars at night!

Walking in the desert is the best medicine of all
