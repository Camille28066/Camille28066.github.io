Research Profile

Climate and environmental researcher with expertise in hydro-climatic variability, remote sensing, and anthropogenic impacts on natural systems. Current work focuses on comparative studies across global cities, integrating in-situ measurements and satellite observations to quantify artificial light at night (ALAN) and analyze the influence of urban morphology on light emissions.
